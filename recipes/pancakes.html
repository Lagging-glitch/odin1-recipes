Pancakes!

Description:

Pancakes are a beloved breakfast staple that bring joy to mornings with their fluffy texture and golden-brown exterior.Whether topped with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a dollop of whipped cream, pancakes are a versatile and delightful way to start the day.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes. Wait until bubbles start to form on the top and the edges look dry and set, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides. Serve and enjoy!
